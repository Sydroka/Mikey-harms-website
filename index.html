About

MIKEY HARMS

MIKEY HARMS is a Chicago-based singer-songwriter known for his heartfelt lyrics and captivating performances. With a passion for music that spans various genres, Mikey brings authenticity and emotion to every song he creates. His journey in music has been marked by dedication to his craft and a deep connection with his audience.

The Band

MIKEY HARMS current line up is Antony on bass, Stevie on drums, Matt on guitar, and Josh on keys.
