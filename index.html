About

MIKEY HARMS

MIKEY HARMS is a Chicagoland singer-songwriter hailing from Tinley Park. Pulling inspiration from across genres and art forms, Mikey is a striking performer with a diverse range - most often aligning with alternative rock and folk. From sentimental tones of simple voice and guitar to mammoth orchestractions, Mikey's sound is the product of eclectic tastes, frequent collaboration, and constant inspiration from his incredible friends and contemporaries. Tenacious and original, Mikey is regarded as a promising and innovative young musician, rising rapidly in the Chicago scene.

The Band

MIKEY HARMS current line up is:

Matt D'Astice on guitar
Josh Picardi on keys
Antony Picardi on bass
Stevie Beilke on drums
